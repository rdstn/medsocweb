Event Descriptions

Sunday Swords - our ever-popular exploration of the European martial arts happens each Sunday. We welcome participants at every skill level and enthusiasm! Currently, the sessions are run by Radostin Nanov.

Crafting Socials - join us on a fine Monday evening and craft the (period) clothes of your dreams. We provide the tools, you need only pay for the materials - we will have a stock of those at the event.

Pubbing Socials - just about what it says on the tin. A tour around York's numerous historic pubs.

Music Social - there will be dancing, there will be singing. If you have a passion for music, this is for you... so long as you also have some strong ears!

Bonfire Social - a calm night around a camp fire, right on one of the green areas of York. There may be some food, but the whimsy is guaranteed.

Banner Social - specific to pre-SwordClash weeks, it's a social devoted to making decoration for the fair.
